Brewing guide

How to dial in a cleaner morning pour-over.

Mot cong thuc tinh gon cho ly coffee sang ro, ngot va co hau vi dai.

5 min read / Lumiere Reserve Journal

Pour-over ngon bat dau tu su on dinh. Neu grind qua min, nuoc di cham va vi de bi chat. Neu grind qua tho, cup mong va thieu do ngot. Hay bat dau o muc medium-fine, sau do dieu chinh tung nac nho.

A clean cup is less about doing more, and more about repeating the right small moves.

Bloom slowly

Dung khoang gap doi luong nuoc so voi ca phe cho lan bloom dau tien. Cho 30 den 40 giay de khi CO2 thoat ra, giup nhung lan rot sau deu va thom hon.

Pour with rhythm

Rot theo vong tron nho, giu muc nuoc on dinh va tranh tac dong qua manh vao thanh filter. Tong thoi gian ly 18g coffee thuong nam trong khoang 2:45 den 3:20.

Finish and taste

Neu cup chua sac va mong, xay min hon. Neu cup nang, chat va kho, xay tho hon hoac ha nhiet do nuoc. Ghi lai tung lan de tim profile hop gu cua ban.
